Pansophy Contact Manager Help:
About Pansophy


Current Version:3.1
Original Authors:Jeremy DeGroot, Joel Wietelmann, and Alex Chvatal
Current Coding Staff:Trisha Fultz, Robert Taylor, and Josh Thomas
Last Update:July 2, 2009

Pansophy Contact Manager is a tool that allows you to easily synchronize records about staff contacts regarding different issues with the student body. It helps you see who is involved with what issues and view student demographics and issue/contact history. Pansophy Contact Manager is an easy-to-use tool for keeping student administrators updated and making sure that no issues fall through the cracks.

Pansophy Contact Manager was created for the College of Wooster Department of Student Affairs through the College's AMRE (Applied Mathematics Research Experience) program. Special thanks goes to AMRE director Dr. John Ramsay, project advisors Dr. Simon Gray and Kathy Breitenbucher, database administrator Michael Thompson, and the entire Student Affairs staff.

For modifying Pansophy for Version 3.0, we would like to also recognize our project advisor Dr. Denise Byrnes, as well as Kurt Holmes and Robyn Laditka for being very cooperative and available as we worked through the bugs and new functionalities.
